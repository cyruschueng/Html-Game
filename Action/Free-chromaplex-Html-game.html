Chromaplex is a colorful action game with simple controls. Just try to remove the moving particles by catching them within a circle drawn by your mouse. Be careful to remove only particles which colors match the playfield background.

Play Instructions:

Catch and remove the colored particles by surrounding them with a circle. To draw a circle simply press and hold the left mousebutton, then move your mouse.
