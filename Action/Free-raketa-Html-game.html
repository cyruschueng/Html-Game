Play Instructions:

To move Rocketman use the left and right arrow keys on your keyboard. When you begin Rocketman is at the launch controller (far right), carry his rocket over to the launcher (far left). Once at the launcher Rocketman will automatically place it on the launcher ready for launch. If Rocketman is hit by a Soviet rocket he will be killed and the game will end. Catch the falling "Golden Rockets" for bonus points.
